Who are we?

Welcome to "Chino Hills Pet Adoption," your local haven for finding your new best friend. Established in 2010, our community-driven organization is committed to rescuing homeless animals and matching them with loving families. Our mission is simple yet impactful: "Saving Lives, Creating Families." Over the past decade, we've successfully placed over 5,000 pets in forever homes, and our work doesn't stop there. We also engage in community education on responsible pet ownership and offer free spay/neuter programs. When you adopt from us, you're not just gaining a pet; you're joining a community committed to animal welfare.


How It works?

Adopting a pet from "Chino Hills Pet Adoption" is a straightforward yet thorough process designed to ensure the best fit for both the animal and the adopter. Here's how it works:

  • Visit Our Center:

    • Drop by to meet our adorable pets and let one catch your eye and heart.

  • Consultation:

    • Speak with our trained adoption counselors to discuss your lifestyle and pet preferences. This ensures we match you with a pet that suits your home environment.

  • Meet & Greet:

    • Spend quality time interacting with your potential new family member in a designated area to assess compatibility.

  • Application:

    • Fill out our adoption application providing details such as previous pet ownership and veterinarian references.

  • Home Check

    • In some cases, a home visit is conducted to ensure a safe and suitable environment for the pet.
